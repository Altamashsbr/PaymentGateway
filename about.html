About Us

HelpingHand is a NGO (Non-Profit Organization) established with the pledge to help poor, needy and Underprivileged Child. It is our vision that all such needy children should be given an opportunity to get Education, Medical & Emotional Care All the Help and Support should be reached to every Underprivileged children. TCHF are having various Programs for children who are in need of Education and Health care
